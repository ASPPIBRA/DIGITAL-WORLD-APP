who we are

ASPPIBRA-DAO We are ASPPIBRA - Association of Property Owners and Possessors in Brazil. We are a non-profit civil association, founded on 09/04/2016, in the State of Rio de Janeiro-RJ, formed by professionals, liberals and citizens who wish to contribute to the socioeconomic development of modern society.

What we do

State-of-the-art rural offices

At ASPPIBRA-DAO, we are wholeheartedly committed to pioneering a forward-thinking approach to technology integration that promises to revolutionize the rural office landscape. Our steadfast mission is to extend the advantages of cutting-edge technology to the core of rural communities, providing them with the indispensable tools and resources essential for thriving in an ever-advancing digital era.
